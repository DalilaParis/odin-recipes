Milanesa

On this page you're going to learn how to make the tastiest milanesa ever made, from Argentina to the world!

The recipe is insanely easy and fast to make, it's a delicious lunch for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken.
  • 2 eggs.
  • Grated Cheese.
  • A spoon of mustard.
  • 200 gr of bread crumbs.
  • A little bit of oil.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs with the cheese and mustard.
  2. Put the pieces of chicken in the mix.
  3. When you're sure they're evenly coated, put them on a bowl full of bread crumbs.
  4. Bury them completely on the crumbs. You can press them down in order to make sure they stick to the chicken.
  5. Put them on a pan with a bit of oil till brown. And you're done!!!

See how easy it was!!!
